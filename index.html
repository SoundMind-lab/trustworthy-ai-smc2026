Artificial intelligence is increasingly deployed in safety-critical environments such as healthcare monitoring, disaster response, and information authenticity verification. This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners working on trustworthy AI systems for safety-critical perception and decision-making.

Important Dates

Acceptance Notification: April 26, 2026
Camera-Ready Submission: May 24, 2026

Call for Papers

We invite original research papers on trustworthy AI for safety-critical systems.

  • Uncertainty estimation and confidence calibration
  • Explainable AI for safety-critical decision making
  • Human-AI trust and interaction
  • Robust perception under noisy or incomplete data
  • Failure detection and anomaly awareness
  • AI for medical and physiological monitoring
  • Disaster detection and damage assessment
  • Deepfake and media authenticity detection
  • Human-in-the-loop learning and decision support
  • Deployment challenges for real-world AI systems

Submission
